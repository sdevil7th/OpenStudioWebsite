One Developer. 3 Months. AI-Powered.

OpenStudio wasn't built in a boardroom. It was forged in the quiet hours between midnight and dawn by a single engineer and a fleet of AI agents. Using advanced LLMs to scaffold complex C++ DSP architectures and automate the tedious boilerplates of the JUCE framework, we compressed three years of development into ninety days.

The result is a DAW that understands the modern workflow: lean, intelligent, and infinitely extensible.
